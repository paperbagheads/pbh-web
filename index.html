Meet the Paper Bag Heads

Collection of 10,000 NFTs

"Paper Bag Heads" (PBH for short) is an NFT art project with unique collectibles that will be minted on the Ethereum blockchain. Each NFT's features were hand-created by us and each NFT was algorithmically created to ensure uniqueness and rarity. There are over 278 million combinations of these features; however, only 10,000 NFTs will be created.

Become a PBH Owner

By minting a PBH NFT or buying one in a secondary sale, you can become a PBH Owner.

PBH Ownership is your entry pass into all the PBH activities, events, and opportunities. To name a few of these, we are currently working on Paper Museum, "PBH Art Piece" (a community driven art project for owners), Members Only Site, super cool merch, and Paper Hunt (we will keep this one secret for now). Along with the projects we are working on, PBH owners are also eligible to vote on community activities and obtain leadership roles.

And of course, you get a SUPER cool SUPER high-res (4320px X 4320px) PBH NFT PFP. You are free to do whatever you'd like with your PBH NFT.
