Meet The PaperBagHeads

Collection of 12,600 NFTs

"PaperBagHeads" (PBH for short) is an NFT art project with unique collectibles that will be minted on the Ethereum blockchain. Each NFT's features were hand-created by us (no external artists - all us) and each NFT was algorithmically created to ensure uniqueness and rarity.

Features

There are over 90 million combinations of these features; however, only 12,600 NFTs will be created.

  • Background
  • Bag Style
  • Handles
  • Skin
  • Eyes
  • Nose
  • Mouth
  • Face Art
  • Borders
  • Special Additions

Become a PBH Owner

Look out for information about minting a PBH NFT! By minting one or more, you become a PBH Owner, which comes with a lot of perks!

PBH Ownership is your entry pass into all the Paper Project activities, events, and opportunities. To name a few of these, we are currently working on Paper Museum (a community driven art project for owners), Paper Merch Store (super cool merch available to owners), and Paper Hunt (we will keep this one secret for now).

Along with the projects we are working on, PBH owners are also eligible to win free prints of their NFT art piece, vote on community activities, and have leadership opportunities.
