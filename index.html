What is "Paper Bag Heads"?

"Paper Bag Heads" (PBH for short) is a NFT collection of 10,000 unique hand-drawn and algorithmically generated paper bags living on the Ethereum blockchain. Because they were created as ERC-721 Tokens, each PBH NFT is unique and non-fungible. Only one person can own a PBH NFT at a time. (See pieces from the collection above)

About The Collection

The art is meant to be simple and creative, taking elements from existing NFT projects like Apes and Punks. Each image is algorithmly created using 4 to 9 attributes to ensure uniqueness and rarity in the collection. There are a numerous amount of options for each attribute, and every option or "feature" was hand-created by us. There are over 278 million combinations of these features. We spent a lot of time trying to come up with features for everyone - whether you like shopping, collecting apes, contemplating going to Mars, or even smoking weed, we are sure you will find a bag that's just right for you.

FAQs

What is an NFT?

NFT ("Non-fungible token") is a truly unique digital item that you can buy, own, and trade.

What is Metamask?

Metamask is crypto-wallet that can hold Ethereum. You will need to it to purchase/mint a PBH NFT. Instructions about how to install it are here: https://metamask.io/.

How can I get a PBH NFT?

You will need to "mint" one (or more) on Ethereum. Be on the lookout for the link to our minting website. You will need to have a Metamask wallet and Ether ($ETH).

First time minting an NFT?

No worries. We're here to help. We'll post how-to videos on our socials and discord so be sure to follow. Anything in specific? Just reach out to us on our socials or discord.

Why should I buy a PBH NFT?

Well, there are really only 2 reasons. Get a super cool profile pic and support our future projects.

What do I do after minting my NFT?

Intially, all PBH NFT images will be hidden. After the collection sells out, we will release metadata & images for all PBH NFTs. You will be able to see all the cool attributes your PBH has and view it on Opensea and in your Ethereum Wallet.
